The data are from Table 2 of Ellison (2004), which in turn are from Gotelli & Ellison (2002).

### variables

site: 22 sites in Connecticut, Massachusetts, Vermont (USA)
habitat: bog or surrounding forest.
latitude: decimal degrees.
elevation: meters above sea level.
richness: number of ant species found in a 64 m^2 sampling grid.

### Citations

Ellison AM (2004) Bayesian inference in ecology. Ecology Letters 7: 509-520.

Gotelli NJ, Ellison AM (2002) Biogeography at a regional scale: determinants of ant species density in New England bogs and forests. Ecology 83: 1604-1609.
